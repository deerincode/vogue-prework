Why Per Scholas?

For years now, I've been determined to start a career in software design and engineering. My initial pursuit of this goal was by going for a bachelors degree in computer science. Unfortunately, I found that this approach to be costly, too slowly paced, and did not feel like it was preparing me for a career. Dropping out from my program at the beginning of this year, I began looking into low-cost/tuition free programs that would offer me the training I needed to not only secure a job in the world of software engineering, but to excel in it. When I disovered Per Scholas, it was immediately clear to me that it was an ideal program for me to apply - not only meeting what I was looking for, but exceeding it.
